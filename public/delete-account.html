✝ Delete Your Account

To request deletion of your Dinner with Jesus account, please email us at:

info@onetengroup.ai

Please include "Account Deletion Request" in the subject line and the email address associated with your account.

What gets deleted: your profile (name, email, faith-level and translation preferences), your journal entries and reflections, your dinner-circle membership, and your login credentials.

What may be retained: we do not keep a copy of your deleted data for our own use. A small amount of information may be kept only where required by law (for example, fraud prevention or legal record-keeping) and never for any other purpose.

Timing: this is not instant. A person reviews and processes each request, and it can take up to 30 days to complete.

If you don't receive a confirmation within that time, or anything about this process doesn't work as described, reply to the same email thread or write to us again — a real person will make it right.
