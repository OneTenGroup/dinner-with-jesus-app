How it works

Simple enough for any family.
Deep enough to change one.

No prep. No homework. No theology degree required. Just show up at dinner and let the table do the rest.

📖
One Verse

A new verse every night — with context, backstory, and a question that actually starts a conversation.

💬
One Conversation

Three levels of questions — exploring, growing, going deeper. Your family picks where they're ready to go.

🙏
One Prayer

A written prayer tied to tonight's verse. Everyone takes a turn. Nobody has to perform — just read the words.

📓
One Journal

Save what happened at the table. Personal journal. Family journal. Something your grandchildren will one day read.

From the table

It happens when you least expect it.

"We took Dinner with Jesus to a restaurant we frequent. Our server asked what we were doing. We explained — and asked if she wanted to pray with us. She sat down right there at our table. She looked down at the app, laughed, and said it was Kendyl's turn anyway. Then she got quiet. She said she really wasn't good at praying. We told her — don't worry, the prayer is already there. Just read the words. She did. Every word. It was one of the most beautiful moments we've ever had at a dinner table."

— Steve & Mandy · Founders, Dinner with Jesus · Frisco, Texas
Why we built this

Built for Maddie.
Meant for every family.

We lost Maddie before we were ready. Before we had said everything we wanted to say. Before we had enough dinners together. Before we had the chance to sit across from her and talk about the things that really matter.

Grief has a way of showing you exactly what you wish you had done differently. More time at the table. More questions asked. More faith shared — not preached, just lived out loud over dinner, the way Jesus intended.

We can't go back. But we can make sure that no other family looks back wishing they had talked more, prayed more, loved more — while they still had the chance.

Dinner with Jesus is for Maddie. And for every family that still has time.
