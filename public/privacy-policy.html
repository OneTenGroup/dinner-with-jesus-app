Privacy Policy

Dinner with Jesus · flippingtables.ai
Last updated: July 1, 2026

At Dinner with Jesus, we believe your family's conversations are sacred. This Privacy Policy explains how OneTen Group ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the Dinner with Jesus app ("the App") available at flippingtables.ai.

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the minimum information necessary to provide you with the Dinner with Jesus experience:

  • Account information: Your name and email address when you create an account.
  • Profile preferences: Your faith level, preferred Bible translation, and dinner circle membership.
  • Journal entries: Notes you save within the app, stored securely in your account.
  • Usage data: Which features you use and when, to help us improve the experience.
  • Device information: Browser type, operating system, and device type for technical support.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect your location.
  • We do not access your contacts, camera, or microphone.
  • We do not sell your personal information to third parties. Ever.
  • We do not show you advertisements.
  • We do not track you across other websites or apps.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

  • Provide and improve the Dinner with Jesus experience
  • Sync your dinner circle, verse history, and journal entries across devices
  • Send you app-related notifications if you opt in
  • Respond to your support requests
  • Send important updates about the App (no marketing without your consent)

4. Dinner Circle Data

When you create or join a dinner circle, other members of your circle can see your display name. Journal entries saved to the family table journal are visible to all members of your circle. Personal journal entries are visible only to you.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a trusted cloud database provider with industry-standard encryption. We use secure HTTPS connections for all data transmission. We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us at info@onetengroup.ai.

6. Children's Privacy

Dinner with Jesus is designed for families. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without verifiable parental consent. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately and we will delete it.

7. Third-Party Services

We use the following trusted third-party services to operate the App:

  • Supabase — database and authentication
  • Vercel — app hosting and deployment
  • Resend — transactional email delivery

Each of these services has their own privacy policies and security practices. We only share the minimum data necessary for these services to function.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Correct any inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Opt out of any communications from us
  • Export your journal entries at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at info@onetengroup.ai.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes through the App or by email. Your continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Email: info@onetengroup.ai
  • Website: flippingtables.ai
  • Company: OneTen Group
